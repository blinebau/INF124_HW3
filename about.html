Bryan Linebaugh: Co-founder

An entrepeneur with a passion for handcrafted artifacts and artisanship. Bryan has a wealth of experience selling of high quality and inspirational products from around the world. Weaves & Crafts is no exception to this, selling new styles of handcrafted clothing from different parts of the world. Partnered with Mike Duong, this new venture is another chapter in quality craftsmanship.

Mike Duong

Fashion designer, traveler, and avant-garde. Mike Duong has learned to combine all these skills to bring you the most desirable fashion trends seen today. By collaborating with Bryan Linebaugh, these ideas are brought from the conceptual to the real. From all around the world specialized artisans have tirelessly and painstakingly worked on their craft and they are finally made ready to be shown on the world stage.
