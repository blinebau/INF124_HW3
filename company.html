Our Company

Founded in 2017 by Bryan Linebaugh and Mike Duong, Weaves & Crafts seeks to enrich the lives of others by providing artisan clothing from cultures around the world. Based on the work completed by artist Mike Duong, Weaves & Crafts sells a selection of worldly items. The products sold are born from the inspiration of international cultures Mike visited over his career. With the support of Bryan, Weaves & Crafts wants to bring Mike's art to reality and share it with the world. Our company makes a promise to sell quality artisan products to customers that will allow them to a experience some of the same inspiration gained by our artists.
